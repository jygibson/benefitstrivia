What are the benefits of a Health Savings Account?

The money goes in pre-tax, which while it makes your take home pay smaller, it's a smaller amount to be taxed.
It's your own savings account, you can keep it forever -- even use it in retirement too!
Once your balance reaches $1,000, you can invest the money, and all dividends are tax free.
Once you have a balance, an HSA can help ease the burden on your wallet created by the larger deductibles.
